Who are we?

In primary and secondary school, Red Cross Youth is a co-curricular activity (CCA) that integrates character-building, leadership, essential life-saving skills, and humanitarian knowledge. It encourages strong moral values and fosters meaningful service to local communities through various humanitarian projects in association with the Red Cross society in Singapore and many countries worldwide.

It brings out the best of our Links (Primary schools), Cadets (Secondary schools), and Chapters (Tertiary members) as we empower them for leadership in their families and communities, equipping them with 21st-century skills to excel in both academic and personal development.
